# Calendar Captures

## Meeting

```text
Meeting with Sarah Monday 10am about partnership
```

Default: 1 hour.

## Appointment

```text
Dentist Tuesday 3pm
```

Default: 1 hour.

## Large event

```text
Dinner with team Friday 8pm
```

Default: 2 hours when context implies a longer block.

## Date-only task

```text
Task: submit invoice Friday
```

Default: all-day.
